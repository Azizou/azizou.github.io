Engineering GPA (UCT) calculation

To access the Term average page, first login to People soft, then:

  1. Go to Self Service -> Academic Record -> Grade Averages from the drop down (Main Menu>Self Service>Academic Records>Grade Averages)
  2. Click on Search without entering any data on the begins with field
  3. Click on the first UGRD link on the result list
  4. Click on Return to View My Grades at the bottom of the page (after the table with some courses)
  5. Voila! You are now on the page with your averages for the current year. Use change term button at the top to get to choose a new academic term
